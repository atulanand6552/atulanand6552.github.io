Common Questions about AI

Artificial intelligence refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans. It's an interdisciplinary science that includes machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing.

AI is not designed to replace human jobs, but rather to augment human capabilities. It will automate repetitive tasks, allowing people to focus on more creative, strategic, and complex problem-solving roles. Staying informed about AI trends is key to adapting.

AI is the broad field of creating intelligent machines. Machine learning is a subset of AI that uses algorithms to learn from data. Deep learning is a more advanced subset of machine learning that uses neural networks to process information in a way inspired by the human brain.
